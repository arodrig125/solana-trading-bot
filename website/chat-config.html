Setup Instructions

Follow these steps to set up your live chat widget

1

Create a Tawk.to Account

Sign up for a free account at tawk.to.

2

Get Your Site ID

Find your Site ID in the Tawk.to dashboard under "Administration" > "Property Settings".

3

Configure Your Widget

Enter your Site ID and customize the appearance and behavior of your chat widget.

4

Add to Your Website

Include the chat-widget.css and chat-widget.js files in your HTML, and update the Site ID in the JavaScript file.

5

Test Your Chat Widget

Visit your website and test the chat functionality to ensure everything is working correctly.
