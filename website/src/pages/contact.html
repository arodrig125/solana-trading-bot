Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How quickly will I receive a response to my inquiry?

Response times vary based on your subscription tier. Basic Plan users typically receive responses within 24 hours, Advanced Plan within 12 hours, and Professional Plan within 4 hours. Enterprise Plan users receive priority response.

I'm having technical issues with my bot. What information should I provide?

When reporting technical issues, please include the following information to help us assist you more efficiently:

  • Your subscription tier
  • Error messages (if any)
  • Steps to reproduce the issue
  • Operating system and Node.js version
  • Whether you're running on local machine or VPS

How do I request a feature or suggest an improvement?

We welcome feature requests and suggestions! Please use the contact form and select "Feedback" as the subject. Describe your idea in detail, including how it would benefit users. Our development team reviews all suggestions and prioritizes them based on user demand and feasibility.

Can I get a demo before purchasing?

While we don't offer a free trial, we do provide a 7-day money-back guarantee for all subscription tiers. This allows you to test SolarBot risk-free. Additionally, we can schedule a demo call for Enterprise Plan inquiries. Contact us to arrange a demonstration.
