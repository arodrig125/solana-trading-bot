Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about SolarBot

How does SolarBot find arbitrage opportunities?

SolarBot continuously monitors price differences across multiple DEXs and token pairs on the Solana blockchain. It uses advanced algorithms to identify triangular arbitrage opportunities (e.g., USDC → SOL → BTC → USDC) and cross-exchange price discrepancies in real-time.

Is my wallet secure when using SolarBot?

Yes, your wallet security is our top priority. SolarBot runs on your own VPS or local machine, and your private keys never leave your environment. We do not have access to your funds or private keys at any time.

What kind of returns can I expect?

Returns vary based on market conditions, your settings, and the subscription tier you choose. Typically, users see opportunities ranging from 0.5% to 3% per trade. However, we cannot guarantee specific returns as cryptocurrency markets are volatile.

Do I need technical knowledge to use SolarBot?

No, SolarBot is designed to be user-friendly. Our setup guide walks you through the process step by step. For Advanced and Professional tier users, we offer personalized setup assistance to ensure you're up and running quickly.

Can I run SolarBot on my own VPS?

Yes, SolarBot can be deployed on any VPS with Node.js support. We provide detailed deployment instructions for popular VPS providers like DigitalOcean, AWS, and Linode. Professional tier users receive dedicated VPS setup assistance.

Is there a free trial available?

We offer a 7-day money-back guarantee for all subscription tiers. This allows you to test SolarBot risk-free and ensure it meets your trading needs before committing.
