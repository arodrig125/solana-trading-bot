Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our pricing and plans

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan later?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your subscription at any time. When upgrading, you'll be charged the prorated difference for the remainder of your billing cycle. When downgrading, the new rate will apply at the start of your next billing cycle.

Is there a free trial available?

We don't offer a free trial, but we do provide a 7-day money-back guarantee for all subscription tiers. This allows you to test SolarBot risk-free and ensure it meets your trading needs before committing.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express) and cryptocurrency payments (SOL, USDC, BTC, ETH). For Enterprise plans, we also accept wire transfers.

What's the difference between simulation and live trading modes?

Simulation mode allows you to test strategies without executing actual trades. The bot will identify opportunities and show you what trades would have been made, but no real funds are used. Live trading mode executes real trades with your funds based on the opportunities identified.

Do I need my own VPS to run SolarBot?

Yes, SolarBot needs to run 24/7 to be effective, so a VPS (Virtual Private Server) is recommended. Professional and Enterprise plans include VPS setup assistance. For Basic and Advanced plans, we provide detailed guides on how to set up your own VPS with providers like DigitalOcean, AWS, or Linode.

What happens if I cancel my subscription?

If you cancel your subscription, you'll continue to have access to SolarBot until the end of your current billing period. After that, your access will be revoked. Your data will be retained for 30 days after cancellation, after which it will be permanently deleted.
